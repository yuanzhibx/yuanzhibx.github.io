框架

Dear x,
I am writing this letter to… / This notice is to…

  • thank you for…/congratulate you for…/apologize for you/do sth, and to
  • offer you suggestions/information about …
  • extend you an invitation to do sth.
  • recommend you sth

Regarding … , there are several points I would like to mention.

  • First ofall, 论点 1.
  • What’s more, 论点 2.
  • In addition, 论点 3.
    For more details, you could refer to the attachment below.

I would be glad if you find the information/suggestions mentioned above helpful.
Please feel free to let me know if you have any further questions.
I would appreciate it if you could take my recommendation /invitation / questions
into consideration. And I am looking forward to your early reply.
Yours truly.
Li Ming

As is illustrated in the above excerpt, the author believes that [名词 / V-ing] is of great significance for [名词 / V-ing]. From my perspective, the above text reveal a currently prevailing tendency among contemporary society.

To examine the root cause of this tendency, certain factors should be specifically pointed out here. First and foremost, just as Karl Marx once said, the economic basis determines the superstructure. Given the shifting economic atmosphere and the state of affairs, it is not surprising for us to witness the above-entioned tendency. What’s more, the phenomenon under discussion could be attributed to the diversity/shift of relevant population’s preferences/interests/values. In addition, social media platforms substantially facilitate the flow and intensification of certain awareness, thus accelerating the transition from an emerging phenomenon into the new reality.

In conclusion, this tendency is of profound inevitability, and will continue to exist in the future. Correspondingly, as the vanguard and successor of our society, we should grasp the pulse of this trend, surf the wave of this era, and actively meet whatever challenges and opportunities it may bring forth.
