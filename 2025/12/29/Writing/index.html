  • offer you suggestions/information about …
  • extend you an invitation to do sth.
  • recommend you sth

Regarding … , there are several points I would like to mention.

  • First ofall, 论点 1.
  • What’s more, 论点 2.
  • In addition, 论点 3.

For more details, you could refer to the attachment below.

② I would appreciate it if you could take my recommendation /invitation / questions into consideration. And I am looking forward to your early reply.

Yours truly.

Writing B

Monkey 模板

As is stated in the above text, the author believes that [复述材料内容].
From my perspective, the text reveals a currently prevailing tendency in modern society.

To examine the root cause of this tendency, certain factors should be specifically pointed out here.

  • First and foremost, just as Karl Marx once said, the economic basis determines the superstructure. Given the shifting economic atmosphere and the state of affairs, it is not surprising for us to witness the above-entioned tendency.
  • What’s more, the phenomenon under discussion could be attributed to the diversity/shift of relevant population’s preferences/interests/values.
  • In addition, social media platforms substantially facilitate the flow and intensification of certain awareness, thus accelerating the transition from an emerging phenomenon into the new reality.

In conclusion, this tendency is of profound inevitability, and will continue to exist in the future. Correspondingly, as the vanguard and successor of our society, we should grasp the pulse of this trend, surf the wave of this era, and actively meet whatever challenges and opportunities it may bring forth.

常考话题

(1)个人成长与发展、品质塑造及价值培养、职场、学业、成功、励志

Everyone is eager for success. The above article has presented some elements of success, such as bravery and viewing failure as a ladder to climb higher. Yet, in my opinion, there are additional vital components contributing to success.

Diligence comes first. Through hard work, we can get rid of ignorance, overcome difficulties, and open our minds to new possibilities. Secondly, whole-hearted devotion is key. Whether it’s a small chore or a major project, we should immerse ourselves fully and stay focused. Finally, perseverance cannot be overlooked. It’s about having the willpower to stick with our goals, even when the going gets tough.

As a whole, success is not an overnight achievement but a long-term process. Throughout this journey, keeping diligence, devotion, and perseverance close will lead us to our desired destination.

(2)人类健康与能源可持续发展、能源危机与应对策略

The above article has introduced various clean energies that bring great convenience to our lives. However, nowadays, an energy crisis is drawing near and endangering human survival.

In fact, currently, the waste of energy in industrial and daily consumption is extremely serious and has become an ingrained habit. In the face of this issue, most countries call for stricter conservation of natural resources. This is indeed an urgent requirement. But just relying on conservation is not enough. Even if we save a large amount of energy, we can only put off the energy crisis, not solve it thoroughly. As far as I am concerned, in the long term, we should develop new energy sources rather than just conserving the existing, limited ones.

It is universally acknowledged that humans are highly adaptable and creative. Since we can make use of resources like oil and metal, why not develop and utilize solar energy, nuclear energy, and other potential energy sources?

(3)校园暴力、心理咨询、教育管理、社会问题

Incidents of gang fights and peer bullying on campus are frequently reported. In light of this situation, to combat campus violence, I believe the most effective approach is to establish a proactive counseling mechanism staffed with caseworkers and psychological experts.

Instead of waiting for violence to occur, we should proactively look for warning signs and identify potential offenders. It is necessary for potential offenders to be obliged to engage in mandatory counseling. Through professional interventions, their violent tendencies can be gradually and systematically addressed. Moreover, this counseling mechanism is also available to potential victims. Typically, fearing retaliation, victims stay silent. With such a mechanism in place, they can readily seek guidance and support.

In summary, campus-based counseling is crucial for preventing, intervening in, and eradicating violence.

(4)文化旅游、文化保护、文化多样性、文化传承、旅游经济

Nowadays, the booming of cultural tourism has caused a heated debate about its impact on culture. Some worry that too much tourism might destroy traditions. Others think it benefits culture as it offers money for cultural protection and spreads local culture.

In my opinion, tourism has both positive and negative effects on culture. On one hand, protecting traditional culture and restoring historical sites need a lot of money, which is a big financial burden for local governments. Tourism can help with this. On the other hand, when culture is used for making money, the government and travel agencies may only focus on profitable cultural projects and overlook those with equal value but less profit. This could harm cultural diversity.

In short, tourism is a double - edged sword for local culture. Thus, we should build a good and beneficial relationship between cultural protection and tourism development.
